Berber lion

The Barbary lion (Panthera leo leo) was a local group of lions which are now extinct in the wild {IUCN}. The Barbary lion was also called the Atlas lion and the Nubian lion. It was a local group, or perhaps subspecies, of the lion.

Scientific name: Panthera leo leo

Average length: 3.5 metre

Average lifespan: 100,000 and 110,000 years

Habitat: North Africa, including the Atlas mountain

Weight: 230 kilograms.

Predators: the Atlas bear.

Diet: carnivore

Prey: Barbary sheep.

The Barbary lion is undoubtedly a fierce and powerful creature, known for its size, muscular build, bite force, and overall strength. With its impressive reputation, it's no wonder that it wears the crown of the most powerful lion subspecies.
